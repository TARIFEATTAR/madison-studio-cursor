# Madison Sub-Agents

This directory contains training profiles for specialized sub-agents that assist Madison.

## Current Sub-Agents

### 1. Quality Assurance Agent (`quality-assurance-agent.md`)
**Purpose:** Pre-delivery quality control and editorial review

**Responsibilities:**
- Review all content before delivery
- Check for banned words
- Verify specificity and proof
- Ensure brand voice alignment
- Validate formatting compliance
- Check factual accuracy

**When to Use:**
- Before any content is delivered to users
- Before content is saved or published
- As final quality gate

---

### 2. Administrative Assistant (`administrative-assistant.md`)
**Purpose:** Executive assistant and document coordinator for Solid.AI

**Responsibilities:**
- Generate and proofread PDF documents
- Manage client email communications
- Handle booking and calendar coordination
- Send appointment reminders and follow-ups
- Ensure alignment across all client touchpoints
- Maintain professional standards in all communications

**When to Use:**
- Client booking requests
- Document generation and delivery
- Email management (confirmations, reminders, follow-ups)
- Calendar coordination
- Administrative tasks that free Madison to focus on creative work

---

## How Sub-Agents Work

Sub-agents are specialized assistants that work alongside Madison:

1. **Madison generates** content
2. **Sub-agent reviews** content
3. **Sub-agent flags** issues
4. **Madison revises** if needed
5. **Sub-agent approves** when ready

---

## Adding New Sub-Agents

Potential sub-agents to add:

2. **Brand Guardian** - Brand consistency enforcement
3. **Strategy Advisor** - Positioning and campaign strategy
4. **Content Analyzer** - Analyze existing content for insights
5. **SEO Optimizer** - SEO optimization for content

Sub-agents can be:
