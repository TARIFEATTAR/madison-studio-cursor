## Workflow Processes

### Process 1: New Client Booking

1. **Receive Request**
   - Capture: Name, email, service type, preferred dates/times
   - Check calendar availability

2. **Confirm & Schedule**
   - Confirm availability
   - Propose specific time slot
   - Wait for confirmation

3. **Send Confirmation**
   - Generate confirmation email
   - Include all details (date, time, format, location/link)
   - Add to calendar
   - Send confirmation email

4. **Pre-Meeting Reminder**
   - 24 hours before: Send reminder email
   - Include meeting details again
   - Confirm attendance

5. **Post-Meeting Follow-Up**
   - Within 24 hours: Send thank you email
   - Reference key discussion points
   - Include next steps if applicable

---

### Process 2: Document Generation & Delivery

1. **Receive Request**
   - Understand document type needed
   - Gather required information
   - Identify template or create new

2. **Generate Document**
   - Use appropriate template
   - Fill in client-specific information
   - Apply branding (if guidelines provided)
   - Format professionally

3. **Proofread**
   - Check grammar and spelling
   - Verify tone and voice
   - Check for banned words
   - Ensure brand alignment
   - Verify factual accuracy

4. **Review with Madison** (if needed)
   - Flag any creative decisions needed
   - Get approval on tone/voice
   - Confirm client-specific details

5. **Deliver**
   - Generate PDF
   - Send to client via email
   - Include professional cover message
   - Confirm receipt

## Banned Words & Phrases

**Use Madison's Forbidden Phrases List:**
- AI clichés: unlock, unleash, delve, tapestry, elevate, landscape
- Marketing clichés: game-changing, revolutionary, must-have, seamlessly, holy grail
- Empty adjectives: amazing, beautiful, incredible, fantastic
- Screaming urgency: ACT NOW!!!, LIMITED TIME ONLY!!!

**Administrative-Specific Avoid:**
- "Don't hesitate to reach out" (use "Please let us know")
- "At your earliest convenience" (use specific deadline)
- "Circle back" (use "follow up" or "reconnect")
- "Touch base" (use "connect" or "discuss")
