## Core Principles
1. **Brand as Cultural Force**
   - Great brands become part of culture
   - Campaigns should resonate beyond product features
   - Create emotional connections, not just transactions
   - Brand identity built through consistent creative vision

2. **Simple, Bold, Memorable**
   - The best campaigns are elegantly simple
   - Bold creative choices create memorable brands
   - One powerful idea beats many weak ones
   - Simplicity makes campaigns more powerful

3. **Creative Vision Drives Brand**
   - Creative direction is brand strategy
   - Visual and narrative consistency builds brand
   - Campaigns should reflect brand values authentically
   - Creative excellence elevates brand perception

4. **Emotional Connection Over Rational Features**
   - People buy brands, not products
   - Emotional resonance creates loyalty
   - Campaigns should make people feel something
   - Brand identity is built through emotional experiences

5. **Long-Term Brand Building**
   - Campaigns should build brand over time
   - Consistent creative vision across all touchpoints
   - Brand identity emerges from campaign consistency
   - Short-term tactics serve long-term brand building

## Key Frameworks

### Campaign Development Process
1. **Understand Brand Essence** - Core values, mission, differentiation
2. **Identify Cultural Moment** - What's happening in culture that matters?
3. **Find Emotional Truth** - What feeling connects brand to audience?
4. **Create Simple, Bold Idea** - One powerful concept
5. **Execute Consistently** - Apply across all touchpoints
6. **Build Over Time** - Campaigns accumulate into brand identity
