## NEW FEATURES HIGHLIGHTED IN THESE SCRIPTS

### 1. Brand Health Score System
**What It Is:** Quantified metric (0-100%) showing brand documentation completeness

**Key Points to Emphasize:**
- Green (90%+) = Excellent, Madison has everything she needs
- Yellow (70-90%) = Good, some gaps to fill
- Red (<70%) = Urgent attention needed
- Updates in real-time as you add brand info

**Where It Appears:**
- Dashboard (top card)
- Brand Health page (full analysis)
- Settings → Brand Guidelines (score indicator)

---

### 2. Visual Standards for AI Image Generation
**What It Is:** Upload photography/design rules → Madison Image Studio enforces them automatically

**Key Points to Emphasize:**
- Upload PDF/DOCX with visual guidelines
- Madison extracts: golden rule, color palette (hex codes), lighting mandates, templates, forbidden elements
- When generating images, Madison references these rules
- Warns you if something breaks brand standards
- Example: "Chrome is forbidden. Try aged brass instead."

**Where It Appears:**
- Settings → Brand Guidelines → "Visual Standards for AI Image Generation" card
- Image Studio (Madison panel shows visual standards context)
- Generate-with-claude edge function (system prompt injection)

---

### 3. Gap Wizard (Interactive Brand Fixing)
**What It Is:** Step-by-step modal to fill missing brand information

**Key Points to Emphasize:**
- Click "Fix This Issue" on any Brand Health recommendation
- Guided form with field explanations
- Character counts ensure detail without overwhelming
- Instant score boost when saved
- Completed recommendations disappear

**Where It Appears:**
- Brand Health page → Recommendation cards → "Fix This Issue" button

---

### 4. Score History Tracking
**What It Is:** Line chart showing Brand Health Score over time

**Key Points to Emphasize:**
- Each point = when you ran an analysis
- Upward trend = progress
- Hover for exact score and date
- Re-run monthly or after major updates

**Where It Appears:**
- Brand Health page → Bottom section

---

### 5. Madison Image Studio (Brief Mention)
**What It Is:** AI product photography suite

**Key Points to Emphasize (save deep dive for later video):**
- Generate brand-aligned product images
- Upload reference product → place in new scenes
- Desert backdrop, golden hour lighting, marble surfaces
- Enforces visual standards automatically
- No expensive photo shoots needed

**Where It Appears:**
- Dashboard → Navigation → "Image Studio"
- Dedicated page `/image-editor`

## SCRIPT READING TIPS

### Pacing:
- Speak slightly slower than normal conversation
- Pause 1-2 seconds between major sections
- Emphasize key numbers (Brand Health Score, percentages)
- Vary tone - excited for features, authoritative for best practices

### Key Phrases to Emphasize:
- "Brand Health Score"
- "Visual Standards for AI Image Generation"
- "Gap Wizard"
- "90% or higher"
- "Madison knows your brand"

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:
- ❌ Speaking too fast (viewers need time to absorb)
- ❌ Monotone delivery (inject enthusiasm)
- ❌ Skipping pauses (let visuals breathe)
- ❌ Over-explaining (trust the script length)
