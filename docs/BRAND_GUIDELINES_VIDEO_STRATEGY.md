# Brand Guidelines Video Strategy
## Recommendation: Use Fictitious Brands for Maximum Educational Value

---

## Why Fictitious Brands Are Better

### ✅ **Educational Advantages**
1. **Perfect Teaching Examples** - You can design brands that demonstrate specific lessons
2. **Clear Contrast** - Side-by-side "good vs. bad" is more impactful than real-world complexity
3. **Common Mistakes** - You can include mistakes that 80% of brands make (vague mission, missing audience, etc.)
4. **Universal Appeal** - Fictitious brands don't feel niche-specific
5. **No Proprietary Risk** - No concerns about revealing client information

### ❌ **Why Real Brands (Tarifa Tarr) Are Less Ideal**
1. **Too Specific** - Your brand might be niche (fragrance/attar) which doesn't resonate with all viewers
2. **Can't Show "Bad"** - You can't show your own brand as the "bad example"
3. **Limited Teaching Value** - Real brands are complex; fictitious brands can be simplified for learning
4. **Proprietary Concerns** - Even if it's your brand, you might not want to reveal all details publicly

---

## Creating the Fictitious Brand Documents

### **Luna & Sage - Complete Brand Guidelines (GOOD)**

```markdown
# Luna & Sage Brand Guidelines

## Mission
To create hand-poured, small-batch candles and home fragrances that transform ordinary moments into rituals of calm. We believe in slow craft, natural ingredients, and the power of scent to shape mood and memory.

## Vision
To become the go-to brand for people who value intentional living and appreciate the art of slow consumption. We're not about trends—we're about timeless scents that become part of your home's story.

## Target Audience
**Primary:** Women aged 28-45, college-educated, living in urban or suburban areas. They value:
- Mindfulness and self-care
- Quality over quantity
- Supporting small businesses
- Aesthetic home environments
- Sustainability (but not preachy about it)

**Psychographics:**
- They read design blogs and follow home decor accounts
- They practice yoga or meditation (or want to)
- They gift thoughtfully, not impulsively
- They're willing to pay $35-65 for a candle that lasts 60+ hours

## Brand Values
1. **Intentional Craft** - Every candle is hand-poured in small batches, never mass-produced
2. **Natural Ingredients** - 100% soy wax, essential oils, no synthetic fragrances
3. **Slow Living** - We encourage people to slow down, not consume faster
4. **Transparency** - We share our process, our suppliers, our story
5. **Timeless Design** - Our aesthetic won't look dated in 5 years

## Voice & Tone
**Voice:** Warm, thoughtful, slightly poetic but never pretentious. Like a friend who knows a lot about candles but doesn't lecture you.

**Tone Spectrum:**
- **Educational:** "Soy wax burns 30% longer than paraffin because..."
- **Inspirational:** "Light this when you need to reset your day"
- **Practical:** "Trim your wick to 1/4 inch before each burn"

**Do Say:**
- "Hand-poured in small batches"
- "60-hour burn time"
- "Essential oil blend"
- "Transform your space"

**Don't Say:**
- "Luxury" (too pretentious)
- "Game-changing" (overused)
- "Revolutionary" (we're not reinventing candles)
- "Amazing" (too generic)
- "Must-have" (let them decide)

## Visual Standards
**Primary Colors:**
- Sage Green: #9CAF88 (primary)
- Cream: #F5F1E8 (backgrounds)
- Charcoal: #2C2C2C (text)

**Photography Style:**
- Warm, golden-hour lighting (never harsh studio lights)
- Natural textures (wood, linen, stone)
- Minimal compositions (not cluttered)
- Lifestyle shots (candles in use, not just product)
- No chrome or metallic surfaces (too modern/cold)

**Forbidden Visual Elements:**
- Chrome or metallic accents
- Harsh studio lighting
- Cluttered compositions
- Trendy filters or effects
- Stock photo aesthetic

## Product Philosophy
Every product tells a story. We don't just sell candles—we sell moments. Each scent is inspired by a place, a memory, or a feeling. Our descriptions should transport the customer, not just list features.
```

### **Modern Living Co. - Incomplete Brand Guidelines (BAD)**

```markdown
# Modern Living Co. Brand Guidelines

## Mission
We sell quality home goods for modern living.

## Target Audience
Everyone who wants nice things for their home.

## Brand Values
- Quality
- Innovation
- Customer Service

## Voice
Professional and modern.

## Visual Style
Modern aesthetic with clean design.
```

**Problems Highlighted:**
- Mission is too vague ("quality home goods" could be anyone)
- Target audience is "everyone" (not specific)
- Values are generic (everyone says these)
- Voice is just "professional" (no examples, no spectrum)
- Visual style is "modern" (no hex codes, no photography guidelines)
